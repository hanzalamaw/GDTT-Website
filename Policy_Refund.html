Refund Policy

1. No Refund Policy

All payments made to Greendome Travel & Tours are final and non-refundable. Once a booking is confirmed and payment is processed, no refunds will be issued under any circumstances.

2. Booking Confirmation

Upon receiving full payment, your booking will be confirmed. Any cancellations or changes requested by the customer after payment will not be eligible for a refund.

3. Exceptions

There are no exceptions to this policy, including but not limited to:

  • Personal emergencies
  • Changes in travel plans
  • Weather conditions
  • Flight cancellations or delays by airlines
  • Government-imposed travel restrictions

4. Travel Insurance Recommendation

We strongly advise customers to purchase travel insurance to cover unexpected cancellations, trip interruptions, or other unforeseen circumstances that may prevent them from traveling.
